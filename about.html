ABOUT

I'm Sunil Tamang, an expedition filmmaker, mountaineer, and aspiring NNMGA/IFMGA Mountain Guide based in Nepal. I create cinematic documentaries that capture the spirit of exploration, high-altitude expeditions, and life in some of the world's most remote mountain landscapes.

My work is shaped by firsthand experience in the mountains. From technical climbing to remote expeditions, I believe the most compelling stories come from those who truly live the adventure. Every frame is driven by authenticity, respect for the environment, and a passion for visual storytelling.

Whether documenting Himalayan expeditions, alpine ascents, or the people whose lives are connected to the mountains, my goal is to tell powerful stories that inspire exploration, resilience, and a deeper appreciation for the natural world.
